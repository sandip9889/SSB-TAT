## âŒ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Negative Endings** - Never end with death, failure, sadness
2. **Passive Hero** - Hero must take action, not wait
3. **Romance** - Avoid love stories completely
4. **Violence** - No revenge, murder, fighting
5. **Incomplete Stories** - Always have Past-Present-Future
6. **Unrealistic** - Avoid superhero scenarios
7. **Too Detailed** - Keep it concise, 60-80 words
8. **Poor Time Management** - Practice with timer
