## Tips for Writing TAT Stories

### Structure (4 minutes):
- **30 seconds:** Understand the picture
- **3 minutes:** Write the story (Past-Present-Future)
- **30 seconds:** Review and ensure positive ending

### Key Elements:
1. **Hero:** Male, 22-24 years, relatable name
2. **Challenge:** Clear problem or situation
3. **Action:** Hero takes initiative, shows OLQs
4. **Outcome:** Positive, constructive result
5. **Impact:** Benefit to others/society

### OLQs to Display:
- **Leadership:** Taking charge, guiding others
- **Courage:** Facing danger/difficulty
- **Determination:** Not giving up
- **Helping Nature:** Supporting others
- **Problem-Solving:** Finding solutions
- **Responsibility:** Taking ownership
- **Team Spirit:** Working with others
- **Initiative:** Starting action
- **Adaptability:** Adjusting to situations
- **Communication:** Expressing clearly
