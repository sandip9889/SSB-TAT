## ğŸ“ Story Writing Formula

### The Perfect Structure:

**PAST (1-2 lines):** What led to this situation?
- Example: "Rahul was preparing for NDA exam but was weak in mathematics..."

**PRESENT (2-3 lines):** What is happening now? What action is the hero taking?
- Example: "He made a study plan, joined coaching, practiced daily for 3 hours..."

**FUTURE (1-2 lines):** What will be the positive outcome?
- Example: "His hard work paid off and he cleared NDA with good marks, making his family proud."

## ğŸ’¡ Quick Story Templates

### Template 1: Overcoming Personal Challenge
"[Name], a [age]-year-old [student/person], was facing difficulty in [challenge]. Instead of giving up, he [action taken]. He [specific steps]. After [time period] of dedicated effort, he [positive outcome]. His [OLQ] helped him succeed."

### Template 2: Helping Others
"[Name] noticed that [someone] was facing [problem]. While others [ignored/watched], [Name] immediately [action]. He [specific help provided]. His [OLQ] made a positive difference in [person's/community's] life."

### Template 3: Emergency Response
"When [emergency situation] occurred, [Name] was [present]. Instead of panicking, he immediately [action 1], [action 2], and [action 3]. His quick thinking and [OLQ] [positive outcome]."

### Template 4: Leadership
"[Name] was leading [team/group] when [challenge] arose. He [analyzed situation], [took decision], and [motivated team]. Through his [OLQ], the team [positive outcome]."
